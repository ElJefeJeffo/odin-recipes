Cheesy Ramen

Description

The go-to dish for every broke college student, but with a cheesy addition! If you have the extra cash to splurge, you can make it nutritional with a few baby carrots and you can add some Flammin' Hot Cheetos on the side.

Ingredients

  • A packet of Maruchan Ramen Noodle Soup with your choice of flavor
  • A slice of Kraft Singles American Cheese

    • (Optional)

  • A few baby carrots
  • 1 ounce of Flammin' Hot Cheetos

Steps

  1. Boil 2 cups of water in a saucepan, add noodles and cook for 3 minutes. Stir occasionally.
  2. Turn off heat add the seasoning and stir well for 1 minute.
  3. Serve when the seasoning is fully dissolved.
  4. Slap the Kraft Single on the ramen while it's hot and enjoy!

    5. (Optional)

  5. On your plate, use the baby carrots to split it into two sides. One for the ramen, the other for the Flammin' Hot Cheetos.

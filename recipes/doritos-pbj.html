Peanut Butter, Jelly, and Doritos Sandwich

Description

I'm sure we've all eaten a PB&J sandwich and a ham & cheese sandwich with Doritos as kids. Some of us may still eat them to this day. But what if we took it a step further and combined what makes the ham & cheese sandwich with Doritos great, which is the Doritos, and combined them with the PB&J sandwich? Well, one person dared to take that step and transcended humanity. And you can too!

Ingredients

  • An 18 ounce bottle of Welch's Natural Concord Grape Spread

  • A 5.56 pound bucket of creamy peanut butter

  • 1 ounce bag of Nacho Cheese Doritos

  • 2 bread slices

Steps

  1. On one slice of bread, spread some peanut butter with a butter knife first.

  2. On the other slice, squirt some jelly and spread it with the same knife.

  3. Open the Doritos bag and spread some across both slices

  4. Finally, find a way to put both slices together and enjoy!
